Find the Gopher

The rules are simple. You have 50/50 chance to find the gopher; one chance on Level 1, two chances on Level 2, etc. For each level you advance, two additional gopher mounds are added. Complete Level 5 to win. There are surprise features as you advance through the game. The only way to find them is to play. Good luck.
